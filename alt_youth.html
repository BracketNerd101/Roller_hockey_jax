Welcome Youth Hockey Players, Coaches, and Parents! This Group is for the Skate Station Youth Hockey League.
Schedules and Rosters will be posted here, as well as schedule and player changes.

What you need to play.(Skater)

  • CSA approved helmet with cage
  • Elbow pads, Gloves
  • Ice hockey pants or inline hockey pants (with or without girdle)
  • Shin pads
  • Inline hockey skate

Hockey Monkey Inline Warehouse

What you need to play.(Goalies)

  • CSA approved goalie helmet and cage
  • Upper body protector, Throat guard
  • Catcher, Blocker, Throat guard
  • Hockey pants, Girdle/Protective Cup
  • Goalie pads, Goalie stick, Roller skates
